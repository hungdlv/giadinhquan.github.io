HOW TO PUT YOUR OWN PHOTOS IN THE DESIGN: 
----------------------------------------
-All photos are placed as "Smart Objects".  Right click on the layer, select "Edit Contents"
-A new window will open with with instructions on where to place YOUR photo.  
-Insert your photo in the exact position and then save.  
-Your design will automatically be updated with your photo on the main advertisement.

-For more information on how to edit "Smart Objects" read this tutorial
http://psd.tutsplus.com/tutorials/tools-tips/tools-tips-smart-objects-and-smart-filters/

HOW TO RESIZE THE SHAPES:
-------------------------
-To resize the shapes without loosing quality:
1) Single click on the Vector Mask Thumbnail
2) Choose the Direct Selection Tool (A) in the Tool Palette
3) Make a selection around the Vector shape.
4) Go To: Edit > Free Transform Path
5) Resize Shape... hold shift while resizing for constrained resize.
